ABOUT BLUEPRINT

Welcome to IIT Delhi’s Entrepreneurship Cell flagship Business Plan Competition 2025! This competition is a platform for budding entrepreneurs to showcase their innovative ideas, business acumen, and strategic thinking with four exciting tracks and competitions in three major cities: Delhi, Mumbai, Ahmedabad and Hyderabad. Blueprint promises to be a melting pot of creativity, innovation, and business brilliance.

Track 1

Sustainability, Energy & Enviroment

Examples: Clean energy solutions, waste management, sustainable agriculture, climate tech, eco-friendly products.

Track 2

Fintech,Bussiness & Consumer services

Examples: Payment gateways, blockchain, B2B SaaS, e-commerce, marketing platforms, consumer apps

Track 3

Health,Education & Social Impact

Examples: Healthcare technology, biotech, medtech, edtech, mental health platforms, social impact enterprises.

Track 4

Deep Tech , AI & Emerging Technologies

Examples: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Quantum Computing, Web3, IoT, robotics
